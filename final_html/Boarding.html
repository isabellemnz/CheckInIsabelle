Fly With Us Airlines

Boarding Information

1. The actual boarding gate number may be varied with that shown in the online check-in system. Please check with the airport Flight Information Display System or information counter for inquiries. Under circumstances such as flight cancellation or combined flight, seat arrangement would be subjected to charge. Please kindly follow the arrangement offered by our airport staff.

2. Passengers who have baggage to check-in, please make sure you do not put laptop computer, cash, or other valuable articles in your baggages.

3. Passenger who have transacted online check-in but could not continue the journey, please cancel the seat one hour prior to flight departure via Fly With Me website www.fwmairlines.com, otherwise would be considered as "on-board".

4. Passengers who have transacted online check-in of international flight, please contact Fly With Me check-in counters in person one hour prior to flight departure to acquire formal boarding pass.

5. To all passengers who will not use web or app check-in, please go to G6, online check-in counter, to get boarding pass.

6. To all passengers, please go to G6 counter, to drop off your baggage.
